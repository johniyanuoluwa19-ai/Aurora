# Aurora Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Aurora is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors and community members.

## Our Standards

We expect all community members to:

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Focus on constructive feedback
- Respect diverse perspectives and experiences
- Accept responsibility and apologize for mistakes
- Report violations responsibly

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- Hateful or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Sharing others' private information
- Any form of abuse

## Enforcement

Violations of this code will result in:

1. Warning from maintainers
2. Temporary suspension from community
3. Permanent ban if severe or repeated

## Reporting

Report violations to **hello@auroraapp.com** with:
- Description of the incident
- Evidence or links
- Your contact information

We will investigate and respond within 48 hours.
